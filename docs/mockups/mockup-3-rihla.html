Words / كَتَبَ
ك
كَتَبَ
kataba
Verb MSA Intermediate Familiar
Translation

to write, to compose, to correspond

Examples

كَتَبَ الطَّالِبُ رِسَالَةً إِلَى أُسْتَاذِهِ

kataba ṭ-ṭālibu risālatan ilā ustādhihi

The student wrote a letter to his professor

تَكْتُبُ كُلَّ يَوْمٍ فِي دَفْتَرِهَا

taktubu kulla yawmin fī daftarihā

She writes every day in her notebook
