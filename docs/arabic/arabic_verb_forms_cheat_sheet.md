# Arabic Verb Forms (Awzan al-Af3al): Comprehensive Grammar Cheat-Sheet

Arabic verb morphology is built upon a system of triliteral roots combined with specific vowel patterns and consonantal affixes. Standard Arabic traditionally recognizes ten productive verb forms, denoted by Roman numerals (I through X). Each form modifies the core lexical meaning of the root in predictable semantic, causative, reflexive, or reciprocal ways.

---

## Overview of Verb Forms I to X

| Form | Pattern (Root k-t-b / f-3-l) | Primary Semantic Function | Transitivity / Voice Shift |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Form I** | fa3ala / fa3ila / fa3ula | Basic lexical meaning of the root | Intransitive or Transitive |
| **Form II** | fa33ala | Intensive, causative, denominative | Usually Transitive |
| **Form III** | fā3ala | Participatory, reciprocal, striving | Transitive |
| **Form IV** | af3ala | Direct causative, entry into a state | Transitive |
| **Form V** | tafa33ala | Reflexive or passive of Form II | Intransitive |
| **Form VI** | tafā3ala | Mutual or reciprocal action, feigning | Intransitive or Reciprocal |
| **Form VII** | infa3ala | Reflexive passive, spontaneous action | Intransitive / Passive-like |
| **Form VIII** | ifta3ala | Reflexive, deliberate action for oneself | Intransitive or Transitive |
| **Form IX** | if3alla | Acquisition of a color or physical defect | Intransitive |
| **Form X** | istaf3ala | Seeking/demanding, deeming an attribute | Transitive or Intransitive |

---

## Detailed Analysis of Each Form

### Form I (Al-Wazn al-Awwal / الفعل المجرد)
* **Structure:** fa3ala (past), -fa3lu/-fa3ilu/-fa3alu (present)
* **Recognition:** Three bare radical consonants with internal short vowels. No prefixes or gemination.
* **Semantic Property:** Represents the base semantic meaning of the root. Can be transitive or intransitive.
* **Example:**
  - Arabic: كَتَبَ (kataba)
  - Transliteration: kataba
  - Translation: he wrote

### Form II (Al-Wazn ath-Thānī / فَعَّلَ)
* **Structure:** fa33ala (past), يُفَعِّلُ (yufa3cilu) (present)
* **Recognition:** Doubling (shaddah) of the middle radical consonant.
* **Semantic Property:** Intensive action, causative, or denominative.
* **Example:**
  - Arabic: كَسَّرَ (kassara)
  - Transliteration: kassara
  - Translation: he smashed / broke into pieces

### Form III (Al-Wazn ath-Thālith / فَاعَلَ)
* **Structure:** fā3ala (past), يُفَاعِلُ (yufā3ilu) (present)
* **Recognition:** Lengthening of the vowel after the first radical (adding an alif after R1).
* **Semantic Property:** Action directed toward another party, mutual interaction, or striving to achieve an action.
* **Example:**
  - Arabic: كَاتَبَ (kātaba)
  - Transliteration: kātaba
  - Translation: he corresponded with (someone)

### Form IV (Al-Wazn ar-Rābi3 / أَفْعَلَ)
* **Structure:** af3ala (past), يُفْعِلُ (yuf3ilu) (present)
* **Recognition:** Prefix alif (hamzat al-qaṭ3) with a fatha, and sukūn on the first radical.
* **Semantic Property:** Direct causative or entering a state.
* **Example:**
  - Arabic: أَخْرَجَ (akhraja)
  - Transliteration: akhraja
  - Translation: he brought out / expelled

### Form V (Al-Wazn al-Khāmis / تَفَعَّلَ)
* **Structure:** tafa33ala (past), يَتَفَعَّلُ (yatafa33alu) (present)
* **Recognition:** Prefix ta- combined with the doubled middle radical of Form II.
* **Semantic Property:** Reflexive or passive counterpart of Form II.
* **Example:**
  - Arabic: تَكَسَّرَ (takassara)
  - Transliteration: takassara
  - Translation: it got smashed / became broken into pieces

### Form VI (Al-Wazn as-Sādis / تَفَاعَلَ)
* **Structure:** tafā3ala (past), يَتَفَاعَلُ (yatafā3alu) (present)
* **Recognition:** Prefix ta- combined with the elongated vowel of Form III.
* **Semantic Property:** Mutual or reciprocal action among multiple parties, or feigning a state.
* **Example:**
  - Arabic: تَعَارَفَ (ta3ārafa)
  - Transliteration: ta3ārafa
  - Translation: they got to know each other mutually

### Form VII (Al-Wazn as-Sābi3 / انْفَعَلَ)
* **Structure:** infa3ala (past), يَنْفَعِلُ (yanfa3ilu) (present)
* **Recognition:** Prefix in- before the three root radicals.
* **Semantic Property:** Reflexive-passive. Spontaneous occurrence or result of an action without an explicit agent.
* **Example:**
  - Arabic: انْكَسَرَ (inkasara)
  - Transliteration: inkasara
  - Translation: it broke / was broken

### Form VIII (Al-Wazn ath-Thāmin / افْتَعَلَ)
* **Structure:** ifta3ala (past), يَفْتَعِلُ (yafta3ilu) (present)
* **Recognition:** Infix -t- after the first radical, accompanied by an initial prosthetic alif (hamzat al-waṣl).
* **Semantic Property:** Reflexive action done for oneself, or deliberate enterprise/acquisition.
* **Example:**
  - Arabic: اكْتَسَبَ (iktasaba)
  - Transliteration: iktasaba
  - Translation: he acquired / gained for himself

### Form IX (Al-Wazn at-Tāsi3 / افْعَلَّ)
* **Structure:** if3alla (past), يَفْعَلُّ (yaf3allu) (present)
* **Recognition:** Initial prosthetic alif and doubling (shaddah) of the final radical.
* **Semantic Property:** Restricted almost exclusively to colors and physical defects or traits.
* **Example:**
  - Arabic: احْمَرَّ (iḥmarra)
  - Transliteration: iḥmarra
  - Translation: it turned red / blushed

### Form X (Al-Wazn al-Āshir / اسْتَفْعَلَ)
* **Structure:** istaf3ala (past), يَسْتَفْعِلُ (yastaf3ilu) (present)
* **Recognition:** Prefix ista- (alif-sīn-tā') preceding the root radicals.
* **Semantic Property:** Seeking or demanding an action, or considering/deeming something to possess an attribute.
* **Example:**
  - Arabic: اسْتَغْفَرَ (istaghfara)
  - Transliteration: istaghfara
  - Translation: he asked for forgiveness

## Quick Reference Summary Table for Recognition

| Form Prefix / Pattern           | Designation | Typical Meaning Shift        |
|:--------------------------------|:------------|:-----------------------------|
| None (Base)                     | Form I      | Core meaning                 |
| Geminated R2 (فَعَّلَ)          | Form II     | Intensive / Causative        |
| Elongated R1 (فَاعَلَ)          | Form III    | Participatory / Reciprocal   |
| Alif prefix (أَفْعَلَ)          | Form IV     | Causative / Transitive shift |
| Ta- + Geminated R2 (تَفَعَّلَ)  | Form V      | Reflexive of Form II         |
| Ta- + Elongated R1 (تَفَاعَلَ)  | Form VI     | Mutual / Reciprocal          |
| In- prefix (انْفَعَلَ)          | Form VII    | Passive / Spontaneous        |
| -t- infix (افْتَعَلَ)           | Form VIII   | Reflexive / Deliberate       |
| Alif- + Geminated R3 (افْعَلَّ) | Form IX     | Colors / Defects             |
| Ista- prefix (اسْتَفْعَلَ)      | Form X      | Seeking / Deeming            |
